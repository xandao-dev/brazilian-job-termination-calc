### Features

* Calculate job termination rights
* Calculates:
  * Early notice
  * Unsanitary
  * Dangerousness
  * Proportional Vacation
  * Proportional 13th salary
  * Extra hours
  * Night hours
  * FGTS
  * INSS
  * Unemployment insurance
  * fine of article 477 of CLT
  * Total
